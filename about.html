About

Hello! My name is Rebecca Favorito and I’m a UX designer based in Columbus, Ohio.

My professional journey has been shaped by a life-long love of learning, insatiable curiosity, and desire to help others learn, grow, and achieve. Before moving into UX, I earned a PhD in history, taught college history courses, and worked in higher education publishing as a project manager and associate development editor. My interest in UX was sparked by my experiences developing content for an e-learning platform—I began to wonder about the whys behind the ways the platform was structured and designed.

My background in education and publishing has contributed to my growth as a UX designer in a number of ways:

Historian

As a historian, I developed an expertise in using critical thinking to analyze potentially biased data and understand the mindset of people very different from me that I now apply to conducting user research, analyzing research data, and developing user personas and related artifacts.

Educator

I am confident communicating my design decisions because of my experience teaching, which honed both my public presentation skills and my ability to distill complex ideas and present them in a way that is most effective for a given audience.

Project Manager

As a project manager and development editor, I gained invaluable experience in working with cross-functional teams, balancing the needs of a wide variety of stakeholders, and working creatively and effectively within constraints defined by clients or based on technical/platform capabilities.

My joy in UX is centered on bringing not only clarity but also a small sense of wonder to the user experience. I’m eager to work on a wide range of projects so I can continue to explore the wide world of UX and grow as a UX designer.
